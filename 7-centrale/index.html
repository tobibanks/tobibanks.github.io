7 Centrale residences

  • Durosimi Etti, Lekki Phase1, Lagos.

Offering a combination of 1bed, 2bed, 3bed apartments, and 4bed duplex penthouses, there is something for everyone who has an eye for luxury, no matter your family size.

The luxury apartment block has distinctive features. The building is equipped with 2 automatic elevators, a gym, an indoor swimming pool, and a relaxation lounge.

It also features constant electric power, steady water supply at adequate pressures, waste treatment, CCTV, cable TV, wired internet, smart home options, car parking spaces, and luscious landscaping.

The building life-cycle has been carefully considered in its evolutionary design, to make use of solar panels, rainwater harvesting, and other sustainable building techniques.
